Y2M

Download YouTube videos direct to your laptop

Video

Features

  • Choose the video quality before you download
  • Video formats include .mp4 and .webm
  • Watch clips saved on to your laptop/desktop in HD
  • Save music as an mp3 to take with you anywhere

How It Works

  1. Search for the video you want
  2. Pick the quality then click download
  3. For mp3 files just download
  4. View the source on Github.
